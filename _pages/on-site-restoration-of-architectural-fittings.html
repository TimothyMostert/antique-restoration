ON SITE RESTORATION OF ARCHITECTURAL FITTINGS

OVER THE YEARS WE HAVE DONE MANY INTERESTING RESTORATIONS OF THIS NATURE.

THESE INCLUDE STAIR BANISTERS, ANTIQUING NEW YELLOWWOOD AND STINKWOOD DOORS, AS WELL AS EXTENSIVE WORK ON A FITTED KITCHEN MADE WITH BEAUTIFULLY CARVED UNITS BELONGING TO AN OLD PHARMACY FROM FRANCE.

A FEW INTERESTING LOCATIONS HAVE BEEN WHEN CALLED IN TO FRENCH POLISH WOOD PANELLED LIFTS OR TO RESTORE THE FINISHES INSIDE A COUPLE OF LUXURY YACHTS!
