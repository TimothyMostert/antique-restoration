THE WONDER OF FRENCH POLISH

THIS VERY BEAUTIFUL BOMBE CHEST OF DRAWERS HAD PREVIOUSLY BEEN OVER RESTORED AND VARNISHED! THIS IS NEVER A GOOD IDEA WITH VENEERED PIECES, ESPECIALLY ANTIQUES WHERE BONE GLUE HAS BEEN USED. THE VARNISH CONTRACTS OVER TIME AND WEAKENS THE BOND BETWEEN THE VENEER AND CARCASS WOOD,CAUSING LIFTING, BUBBLING AND ULTIMATELY LOSS OF VENEERS AND INLAYS.

ONE OF THE MANY MARVELS OF FRENCH POLISH IS THAT IT IS COMPLETELY COMPATIBLE WITH BONE GLUE AND DOES NOT CAUSE DAMAGES TO VENEERS.
