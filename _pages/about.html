OUR STORY

WE HAVE BEEN ADDING VALUE AND BEAUTY TO AN OUTSTANDING VARIETY OF PERIOD ANTIQUE PIECES AND FINE CONTEMPORY FURNITURE SINCE 1989.

HAVING STARTED OUR BUSINESS DURING THE EIGHTIES ANTIQUE BOOM, OUR TRAINING WAS IN AND OUR EMPHASIS WAS ON CONSERVATIVE RESTORATION AND CONSERVATION. HOWEVER, SINCE THEN INTERIOR STYLES HAVE BECOME SO MUCH MORE ECLECTIC AND INCLUSIVE. WE HAVE APPLIED OUR EXPERTISE AND DEVELOPED NEW SKILLS TO INCLUDE 20TH CENTURY AND MODERN QUALITY FURNITURE. MANY VARIED AND INTERESTING PROJECTS ALSO CONTINUE TO COME OUR WAY, REQUIRING CREATIVE, “OUT OF THE BOX” SOLUTIONS! THESE HAVE INCLUDED REPAIRS TO OLD PAINTED FINISHES, GILDING, ANTIQUING TECHNIQUES, ARCHITECTURAL FITTINGS AND MORE. HAVE A LOOK IN OUR GALLERY.

OUR ON SITE RESTORATION AND REPAIR SERVICE IS VERY USEFUL FOR PIECES THAT ARE TOO LARGE TO MOVE OR FOR CLIENTS WHO WANT US TO GIVE A WHOLE COLLECTION OF PIECES A NEW LEASE ON LIFE. THIS WORK INCLUDES IMPROVING UNSIGHTLY WEAR AND TEAR DAMAGES THROUGHOUT THE HOME.

WE ARE COMMITED TO UNCOMPROMISING QUALITY OF WORKMANSHIP IN OUR RESTORATION AND REPAIRS, HAVING WORKED FOR MANY FINE ANTIQUE DEALERS, DECORATORS, PASSIONATE PRIVATE COLLECTORS AND LOVERS OF FINE MODERN FURNITURE. OUR CLIENTS CONTINUE TRUSTING US TO OFFER A PERSONAL SERVICE OF THE HIGHEST QUALITY.

OUR APPROACH INCLUDES BEING MINDFUL OF THE TASTE AND WISHES OF EACH CLIENT, AS WELL AS THE SPECIFIC RESTORATION REQUIREMENTS OF EACH INDIVIDUAL PIECE.
