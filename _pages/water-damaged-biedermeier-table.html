WATER DAMAGED BIEDERMEIER TABLE

BIEDERMEIER REFERS TO A STYLE OF CENTRAL EUROPEAN FURNITURE FROM THE FIRST HALF OF THE 19TH CENTURY.

WATER HAD DAMAGED THE EBONY INLAY AND STRINGING. CARE WAS TAKEN TO STABILIZE THE LOOSE BITS AND REPLACE THOSE MISSING. MANY SECTIONS OF VENEER WERE LOOSE ON THE BASE.

ONCE THE TABLE WAS OILED AND FRENCH POLISHED, IT WAS BACK TO ITS FORMER GLORY!
