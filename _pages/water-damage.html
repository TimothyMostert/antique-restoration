WATER DAMAGED BURR WALNUT CHEST OF DRAWERS

AN UNFORTUNATE ACCIDENT! DAMAGED VENEER WAS CUT AWAY AND A MATCHING VENEER PATCH WAS LAID TO FOLLOW THE CURVES AND PATTERNING OF THE BURR WALNUT. THIS SHAPING OF THE PATCH HELPS IT TO “MIRACULOUSLY” DISAPPEAR! THE CROSS BANDED EDGE WAS REPLACED WITH MATCHING STRAIGHT GRAINED WALNUT.
