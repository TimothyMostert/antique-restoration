A FINE COLLECTION OF CARVED, PAINTED AND GILDED ITALIAN FURNITURE

THESE PIECES ARE PART OF A VASTE COLLECTION OF ITALIAN FURNITURE COLLECTED OVER THE YEARS. THE TERRIBLE TALE IS THAT THE CLIENT LOST MANY OF HER PIECES IN A FIRE AND WE RESTORED THOSE THAT COULD BE SAVED.

FIRST ALL THE CRACKLED, BUBBLED AND FLAKING FINISHES HAD TO BE PAINSTAKINGLY REMOVED. WE THEN GOT ON WITH THE JOB OF RESTORING THE GILDED AND PAINTED FINISHES AND MAKING SURE THEY WERE THEN “AGED” AS BEFORE.
