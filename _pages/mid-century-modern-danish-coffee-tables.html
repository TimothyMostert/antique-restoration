MID CENTURY MODERN DANISH COFFEE TABLES

THIS SUPERB PAIR OF COFFEE TABLES BY JOHANNES ANDERSEN SILKEBORG HAD EXTENSIVE LIGHT DAMAGE TO THE OLD VARNISH FINISH.

THE CLIENT WANTED A STRONG VARNISH FINISH THAT WOULD MANAGE HOT MUGS AND WET DRINKS!

WE CHOSE TO USE A MARINE VARNISH WHICH WE BUFFED TO A SOFT SHEEN.

FEEDING THE DRY WOOD WITH A GOOD FEEDER BEFORE APPLICATION BROUGHT THE CONTRAST AND TONES BACK TO LIFE
