CONSERVING YOUR ANTIQUE FURNITURE

THE BEST THING YOU CAN DO FOR YOU ANTIQUE FURNITURE IS TO FEED THE RAW CARCASS WOOD USING A MIXTURE OF NATURAL OILS, TREAT FOR WORM INFESTATION AND PROTECT FROM DIRECT SUN AND WATER.
