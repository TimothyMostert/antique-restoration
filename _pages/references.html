REFERENCES

IN ALL THE 24 YEARS THAT I RAN BYGONES ANTIQUES IN CONSTANTIA, CAPE TOWN, HUGH LANE WAS THE BEST RESTORER I EVER WORKED WITH. WE DEALT IN ALMOST EVERY KIND OF ANTIQUE ENGLISH AND FRENCH FURNITURE, FROM 18TH C (AND EARLIER) OAK AND WALNUT, RIGHT THROUGH THE AGE OF MAHOGANY AND ON THROUGH TO CURVY VICTORIAN AND NAPOLEON 111 – AND HUGH WAS EQUAL TO IT ALL. AS THE FASHION FOR ANTIQUE PAINTED FURNITURE TOOK HOLD, VERNA JOINED HUGH’S WORKSHOP TO WORK WITH ANTIQUE PAINTED SURFACES AND GILDED ITEMS, WORKING TO THE SAME HIGH STANDARDS.

ONE OF THE THINGS I MOST ENJOYED ABOUT WORKING WITH HUGH IS HIS LIGHTNESS OF TOUCH IN RESTORATION – ALWAYS KNOWING WHEN TO STOP, UNDERSTANDING THE DELICATE ART OF MAKING A PIECE LOOK LOVED AND CARED FOR, BUT NOT BRAND NEW. WE ALSO APPRECIATED THE EFFICIENCY WITH WHIC THE WORKSHOP IS RUN – ONCE HUGH COLLECTS AN ITEM FROM YOU, HE ALWAYS DELIVERS WITHIN THE PROMISED TIMEFRAME – THERE’S NO QUESTION OF YOUR EXPENSIVE PIECE OF FURNITURE MOULDERING AWAY IN A CORNER UNTIL SOMEONE GETS ROUND TO IT, A VERY COMMON PRACTICE ELSEWHERE...

WHETHER YOU’RE A PROFESSIONAL IN THE ANTIQUE TRADE, OR A PRIVATE COLLECTOR, I’M SURE THAT HUGH LANE AND HIS ANTIQUE WORKSHOP WILL DELIVER THE BEST RESTORATION IN SOUTH AFRICA.

-SHEILA BOARDMAN PAST CHAIRMAN OF THE SA ANTIQUE DEALERS ASSOCIATION
