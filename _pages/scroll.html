CARVING A MISSING VICTORIAN SCROLL FOOT

THE MISSING FOOT WAS CUT AND GLUED IN WITH A METAL PIN. AFTER CARVING IN THE DETAIL IT WAS COLOUR MATCHED AND FRENCH POLISHED. FINALLY A BIT OF ‘DIRT’ WAS APPLIED TO MATCH THE REST OF THE CHAIR.
